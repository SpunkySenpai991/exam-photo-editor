Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool really free?

Yes! This tool is 100% free to use with no hidden charges, no registration required, and no watermarks on your images.

Are my photos safe and private?

Absolutely. All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your photos never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server. We don't store or see your images.

What if my file size is too large after processing?

Use the quality slider to reduce the image quality. Lower quality means smaller file size. Start at 80% and go lower if needed. Most exams accept 70-80% quality without visible difference.

What image formats are supported?

You can upload JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WebP images. The output format is automatically set based on the exam requirements (usually JPG).

Can I process just a photo without a signature?

Yes! After selecting your exam, choose "Photo Only" or "Signature Only" to process just one image.

Why is my photo getting cropped?

Each exam has specific dimension requirements. The tool scales and centers your image to fit these dimensions. For best results, use a photo with your face centered and some space around it.

Does this work on mobile phones?

Yes! This tool works on all devices - smartphones, tablets, and computers. You can even take a photo directly from your phone camera.

My exam is not listed. What should I do?

Contact us at aditya7400jha@gmail.com or reach out on social media (links below) with the exam name and photo/signature requirements. We'll add it to the tool.
